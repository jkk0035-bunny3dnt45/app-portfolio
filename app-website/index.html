About Me

Hi, I'm Jacob Klemick. I'm a passionate media interactive developer with a love for creating innovative solutions and exploring new technologies. I'm constantly engaged in various projects.

Currently, I have just completed my studies at Ara Institute of Canterbury and have obtained a Bachelor's of Information and Communication Technologies in 2024.

I have experience in cross-platform mobile development, two years working with the Unity game engine, and I have recently worked on a large-scale Unity project I am also interested in further enhancing my data analyst and geospatial analyst skills.

In my spare time, I like to do 3D printing and product design. With one project, I created my own Mini-ITX computer case.
